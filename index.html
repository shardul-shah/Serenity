About

I started this website because of many reasons. Here are some of the main ones:

  • To learn and improve in web development by working on a practical, albeit simple, project.
  • To help people improve their focus and concentration on tasks, after realizing such sounds were very useful for myself when studying in university.
  • To help people sleep, relax, or meditate, after reading about the potential use of various kinds of background white noise in both relaxation and concentration.
